## What is Fenado AI?

Fenado AI is an AI-powered platform that converts your ideas into fully functional apps and websites.

## What types of apps / websites can I create?

You can create any type of mobile app or website - from business apps and social platforms to e-commerce solutions and personal projects.

## Do I need programming knowledge?

No, you don't need programming knowledge. You can create professional working apps and websites by simply describing your idea.

## What do you mean by "prototype"?

A prototype is a set of interactive screens that show how your app / website will look. You can design these screens by chatting with AI, instantly see changes, and easily share them with anyone to get feedback.

## How does Fenado AI create my app / website?

Fenado AI transforms your ideas into real, working apps and websites that are ready for your users. We handle all the technical complexity behind the scenes, so you can focus on growing your business.

## What's included in the Free plan?

With our Free plan, you can create one mobile app and one website using our AI platform. It's the perfect way to experience the power of Fenado AI and bring your first digital project to life without any cost.

## What's included in the Business Plus plan?

The Business Plus plan lets you create unlimited functional apps & websites using Fenado AI. You get up to 100 hours of monthly dedicated tech support during business hours, custom APIs & backend development, a dedicated account manager, and priority support. It's ideal for early-age startups.

## What extra features do I get with the Business Express plan?

The Business Express plan includes everything in the Business Plus plan, along with a dedicated tech support (up to 720 hours monthly), a dedicated engineering team, custom integrations, enterprise features, and advanced security. It's perfect for fast-growing startups needing comprehensive support.
