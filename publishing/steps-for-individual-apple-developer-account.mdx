Follow these steps to safely grant publishing access.

# Create Bundle ID

## Step 1: Login to Apple Developer Account
1. Go to [Apple Developer Account](https://developer.apple.com/account)
2. Sign in with your Apple ID

## Step 2: Create Bundle ID
1. Reach out to [hello+publish@fenado.ai](mailto:hello+publish@fenado.ai) to get a bundle ID
2. Go to [Create Bundle ID](https://developer.apple.com/account/resources/identifiers/bundleId/add/bundle)
3. Enter the following details:
- Description: `YOUR APP NAME`
- Bundle ID: `BUNDLE ID obtained from Fenado AI Team`
4. Select the following capabilities:
- ✓ Push Notifications
- ✓ Sign In with Apple

Click "Continue"
![Add new bundle ID](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/cades-dev.appspot.com/o/public%2Fdocs-publish-appstore-create-bundle-id.png?alt=media&token=0eb059df-09c6-43e3-9887-76e58ac85c2a)

5. Click "Register"

# Publishing to App Store

## Step 1: Access App Store Connect
1. Go to [App Store Connect](https://appstoreconnect.apple.com)
2. Sign in with your Apple Developer account

## Step 2: Create a new App
1. In the top menu, click "Apps"
2. Click the "+" button to add a new app
![Add new app](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/cades-dev.appspot.com/o/public%2Fdocs-publish-appstore-1-add-app.png?alt=media&token=d877fa57-e994-4e75-be12-7811875251c4)

3. Enter these details:
    - Platforms: `iOS`
    - Name: `YOUR APP NAME`
    - Primary Language: `YOUR PRIMARY LANGUAGE`
    - Bundle ID: `BUNDLE ID created in the previous step`
    - SKUs: `SKU obtained from Fenado AI Team`
    - User Access: `Full Access`

Click "Create"
![Enter app details](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/cades-dev.appspot.com/o/public%2Fdocs-publish-appstore-create-app.png?alt=media&token=375cd3c1-1948-4d90-996c-8bbe15315cd0)

## Step 3: Add Fenado AI Agent as a User
1. In the top menu, click "Users and Access"
2. Click the "+" button to add a new user
![Add new user](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/cades-dev.appspot.com/o/public%2Fdocs-publish-appstore-1-add-user.png?alt=media&token=14d01029-3d35-4fc5-beb8-10e520bbd371)
3. Enter these details:
    - First Name: `Fenado`
    - Last Name: `AI`
    - Email: `publisher-ai-agent-05@fenado.ai`
    - Role: `App Manager`

Then click "Next".
![Add user role](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/cades-dev.appspot.com/o/public%2Fdocs-publish-appstore-1-add-roles.png?alt=media&token=ab078bcb-b407-45bf-9c55-94a3672cb48d)

4. In "App Access for Fenado AI", only select the new app
![Select app](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/cades-dev.appspot.com/o/public%2Fdocs-publish-appstore-1-add-user-select-app.png?alt=media&token=d375f4eb-a468-4c30-b188-b4733a8c3aad)

5. Click "Invite" to send the invitation

## Step 3: Verify Access
You can directly contact your dedicated account manager or [WhatsApp](https://chat.whatsapp.com/EbDj7Txf9VR0GrICAFpjRk) or email [hello+publish@fenado.ai](mailto:hello+publish@fenado.ai) to verify access

<Note>
Note
  - You maintain complete control of your Apple Developer account
  - The publisher can only manage app submissions and updates
  - These permissions are necessary for automated app deployment
  - Contact [support](https://chat.whatsapp.com/EbDj7Txf9VR0GrICAFpjRk) if you need help with permission settings
</Note>

## Step 4: Sit Back and Relax
Fenado AI will handle everything needed to publish your app to the App Store:
- Generate app bundle
- Create App Store listing
- Add screenshots and descriptions
- Configure app metadata

We'll share a preview of the listing for your review before publishing.

## Step 5: App Published ⭐
After your approval, Fenado AI will submit the app for App Store review. Once approved by Apple, your app will be live on the App Store!

<Note>
The App Store review process typically takes 24-48 hours. We'll keep you updated on the review status.
</Note>
