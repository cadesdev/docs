---
title: 'Accept Apple Developer Agreement'
description: 'Steps to accept the required Apple Developer agreements'
---

Before publishing your app, you need to accept the Apple Developer Agreement on your account. Follow these steps:

## Primary Agreement
1. Go to [Apple Developer](https://developer.apple.com/)
2. Sign in with your Apple Developer account credentials
3. If prompted, review and accept the developer agreement
![Accept Developer Agreement](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/cades-dev.appspot.com/o/public%2Fdocs-publish-appstore-1-accept-agreement.png?alt=media&token=f4ed9f97-e4dc-486d-b6a2-1cb4b9b2d1e4)
4. If no prompt appears, then try visiting, review and accept any pending agreements:
 - [Agreements page](https://developer.apple.com/membercenter/index.action#agreements)
 - [iTunes Connect](https://itunesconnect.apple.com/WebObjects/iTunesConnect.woa/ra/ng/)
