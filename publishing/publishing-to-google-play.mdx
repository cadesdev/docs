# Adding Publisher Access to Google Play Console

Follow these steps to safely grant publishing access.

## Step 1: Access Google Play Console
1. Go to [Google Play Console](https://play.google.com/console)
2. Sign in with your developer account

## Step 2: Invite User
1. In the left menu, click "Users and permissions"
2. Click the "Invite new users" button
![Invite new user](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/cades-dev.appspot.com/o/public%2Fdocs-publish-playstore-1-add-user.png?alt=media&token=912d1bf3-57c0-4e3c-a76b-c43ddc735630)

3. Enter the publisher email: `publisher-ai-agent-05@cades.dev`
4. Click "Account Permissions"
![Add user email](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/cades-dev.appspot.com/o/public%2Fdocs-publish-playstore-1-add-email.png?alt=media&token=101d8a78-85f3-44b7-8611-f586a9427e6f)
5. Select the following 6 permissions:
   - ✓ View app information and download bulk reports (read-only)
   - ✓ Create, edit, and delete draft apps
   - ✓ Release to production, exclude devices, and use Play App Signing
   - ✓ Release apps to testing tracks
   - ✓ Manage testing tracks and edit tester lists
   - ✓ Manage store presence
![Permissions_1](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/cades-dev.appspot.com/o/public%2Fdocs-publish-playstore-1-add-permissions-1.png?alt=media&token=36f9fec7-5ed8-4a43-b164-414190336dac)
![Permissions_2](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/cades-dev.appspot.com/o/public%2Fdocs-publish-playstore-1-add-permissions-2.png?alt=media&token=bae7510d-0ad0-49bb-891f-643f35ffc119)
![Permissions_3](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/cades-dev.appspot.com/o/public%2Fdocs-publish-playstore-1-add-permissions-3.png?alt=media&token=56a13106-95ae-4156-88ad-6d1c8f51aad7)
6. Click "Invite user" button on the bottom right

## Step 4: Verify Access
You can directly [whatsapp](https://chat.whatsapp.com/EbDj7Txf9VR0GrICAFpjRk) or send email to [hello+publish@cades.dev](mailto:hello+publish@cades.dev) to verify access

<Note>
Note
  - You retain full control of your account
  - The publisher can only create, edit and delete draft apps, and release to production
  - These permissions allow for app updates while maintaining your account security
  - Contact [support](https://chat.whatsapp.com/EbDj7Txf9VR0GrICAFpjRk) if you need clarification on permission settings
</Note>

## Step 5: Sit back and relax
Cades will create everything required to publish your new app to Google Play Store (e.g., app file, store listing with screenshots, description, etc.)
We will share a preview of the listing, so you can review and approve it before publishing.

## Step 6: App Published ⭐
Once you approve the listing, Cades will publish the app to Google Play Store.
